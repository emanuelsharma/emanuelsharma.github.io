Hexagons

Hexagons is a competitive turn-based strategy game that takes place on a procedurally generated grid. Players win the game by building mining camps and mechs to maneuver past enemy lines and destroy the enemy base.

The game features a local/online multiplayer mode in addition to AI enemies and campaigns. Moreover, the art is all original and animated procedurally.
